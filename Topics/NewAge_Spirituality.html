New Age & Spirituality

A Christian Perspective on New Age Practices

In the realm of spirituality, there are diverse paths that individuals may choose to explore. Practices such as tarot card reading and yoga, often associated with the New Age movement, have gained popularity in recent years. From a Christian perspective, it's essential to approach these practices with discernment. The Bible encourages believers to "test the spirits to see whether they are from God" (1 John 4:1). This means evaluating spiritual practices and teachings in light of biblical truth.

Tarot Cards

Tarot cards, used for divination, can be seen as conflicting with Christian teachings. The Bible warns against attempts to predict the future outside of God's revelation (Deuteronomy 18:10-12). Christians are encouraged to seek guidance from God and His Word.

Yoga

Yoga, originally a practice with spiritual roots in Hinduism, has been adapted in the West primarily as a form of exercise. If the focus is solely on the physical aspect, some Christians might find it acceptable. However, if yoga is used as a spiritual practice, it could conflict with Christian beliefs. In conclusion, while it's important to respect the diversity of spiritual practices, Christians are encouraged to discern and ensure their practices align with biblical teachings.
